Claim By Action in Widget

Overview

Quikly's script tag can render an embedded widget at the bottom of your site that can incentivize an action, such as providing an email address, answering a survey, watching a video, or following you on instagram. When the visitor completes that action they are rewarded. Quikly tracks the response and reveals the reward.

Scenario

A brand would like to grow their email database and get the email addresses of visitors to their blog. Over a period of a few days, the first 1000 people to provide their email address will get a 10% off offer.

Set Up

Activation URLs

If desired, Quikly can provide activation URLs. These links will activate the campaign for that visitor and aid in tracking the performance of each channel. This can be used to limit participation in a campaign only to a select audience that has visited your site through a specific link.

Alternatively, the promotion can be configured to be available to all site visitors rather than a those that activated through a special link.

Script Tag

The Quikly script tags must be present on your site to dynamically display promotion details, such as instructions on how to complete the action, or how to take advantage of the offer once the action is complete. This tag does not have to be present on every page on your site, but it does have to be included on any page where: a) an activation URLs redirects or b) Quikly renders dynamic content.
